A little about me.

I was born and raised in the Bay Area and am currently studying Cognitive Science with a Specialization in Design & Interaction at UC San Diego.

Though there is some truth in the age-old notion that “form follows function”, I believe that form’s relation to the principles of human cognition is emerging to the forefront

Whether it be a 36-hour design-a-thon or quarter-long project, I view challenges as opportunities to gain in-depth knowledge about various communities and grow my skills as a designer.

In my free time, I enjoy drawing, jewelry-making, and crochet.

Lets work together!

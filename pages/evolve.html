The Task

Background

Identifying the Problem

How might we help prospective EV Buyers become more informed on technical and financial aspects of owning an EV in order to facilitate their transition from gas-powered to electric vehicles?

Moreover, how could we help current owners and prospective buyers connect with each other to create a community in order to help facilitate the global transition to EVs?

Problem Statement

User Research

  1. Personas

    2. Based on our online research and community observations, we created primary and secondary personas based around the Prospective Buyer and Current Owner. The final versions of these personas were developed further through observations from user interviews in order to pinpoint the the needs, wants, and behavioral traits of our target users.

  2. Storyboarding

    3. After identifying our stakeholders, we formulated storyboards that illustrated scenarios that our users might face by establishing the context, the problem, our solution, and the resolution. This process enabled us to map out different features and visually communicate how our app would be address the pain points detailed in our personas. Furthermore, showing these storyboards during our user interviews allowed us to gain helpful feedback as to how realistic and applicable these situations and solutions were to daily life.
